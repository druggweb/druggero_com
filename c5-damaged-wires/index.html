Photos taken at Robins AFB GA

We found these damaged avionics bus wires on not just one aircraft but all aircraft on station. When we brought this up to management they did nothing. We even came up with a fix and our supervisors and managers still did nothing. Five months later when the test flight aircrew happened to find out about it they got something done. It made us feel good that this was fixed but made us loose faith in our leaders.
